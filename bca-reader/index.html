Turn a 96-well BCA plate into protein concentrations and a gel loading plan — from your plate reader's readings, or a phone photo.

1 Load a plate

The accurate path. In your spreadsheet, select just the block of OD readings — one cell per well, laid out like the plate — then copy and paste it here. A column-number header row or a row-label / temperature column can come along; they get dropped and you'll be told. Blank cells become empty wells. You'll tag the plate the same way as a photo, minus the corner marking.

Reads about 5% high against the corrected truth, and one sample on the validation plate is off 14% from a lighting gradient. Good enough to explore with; use the plate reader data when you have it.
